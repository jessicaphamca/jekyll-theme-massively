Through this portfolio, you’ll find examples of my work in areas like: Data Analysis: Extracting meaningful insights from datasets using statistical methods, visualizations, and storytelling.
Business Analytics: Leveraging data to inform decision-making, optimize processes, and uncover opportunities for growth.
