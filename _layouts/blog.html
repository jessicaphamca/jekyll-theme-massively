Generating Synthetic Datasets with Synthpop

This project explores the use of the Synthpop package in R to generate synthetic datasets that preserve data privacy while maintaining statistical accuracy. Analyzing the distribution of continuous variables, compare logistic and linear regression models before and after synthesizing, and evaluate the performance of generating datasets of varying sizes. While synthetic data effectively covers missing values and maintains overall distributions, challenges remain in recognizing specific categories and accurately handling outliers. Solutions may require additional rules and access to original data descriptions.
